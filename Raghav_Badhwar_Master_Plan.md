## 4. TECH STACK & INTEGRATIONS (THE "SECRET SAUCE")

We use the **"T3 Stack on Steroids"** with specialized AI/Identity modules.

### **Frontend (Performance & UX)**
*   **Framework:** **React Native (Expo)** for Mobile (iOS/Android) | **Next.js 14** (App Router) for Web.
*   **Styling:** **Tailwind CSS** + **Framer Motion** (for "wow" interactions).
*   **State:** **Zustand** (lightweight) + **TanStack Query** (data fetching).

### **Backend (Scale & Security)**
*   **Core:** **Bun** or **Node.js** (Fastify) for microsecond latency.
*   **Language:** **TypeScript** (Strict Mode).
*   **Database:** **PostgreSQL** (Supabase) for structured data + **Redis** (Upstash) for hot caching.
*   **AI Ops:** **Python (FastAPI)** services for ML inference.

### **AI & ML Layer (The Moat)**
*   **OCR:** **Azure AI Document Intelligence** (better than Tesseract).
*   **Fraud Detection:** Custom **XGBoost** model trained on fraud patterns.
*   **Liveness:** **FaceTec** or **Mediapipe** (Google) for 3D face mapping.
*   **LLM:** **OpenAI GPT-4o-mini** for "Resume Parsing" and "Skill Extraction".

### **Integrations (The Ecosystem)**
*   **Government:** **DigiLocker API** (Setu / Sandbox).
*   **Payments:** **Razorpay** / **Cashfree** (Subscription automation).
*   **Messaging:** **WhatsApp Business API** (Critical for India).
